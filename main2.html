Rules of the game

General concepts

In the game "Bulls & Cows" are two players(one of which is a computer)

At the beginning of the game, the first player makes a number from non-repeating numbers. During the game, the second player tries to guess the number. To do this, he calls another number from non-repeating numbers, and the first player tells him the number of "bulls" and "cows"

The number of "bulls is the number of guessed figures standing at the right places
The number of "cows" is the number of guessed figures that are not in the right places

For example, let the first player make the number "3219", and the second player offers the number "2310". In this case, the result will be 1 bull (the number "1" is at the right place) and 2 cows (the numbers "2" and "3" are on the wrong places)

How to play?

If you want to guess the number:

Choose the difficulty of the game. To do this, click on the button with the number 4, 5 or 6. The number on the button indicates the length of the intended number.
In the field "Enter the number:" enter the number from non-repeating numbers you expect. Below will be the history and results of your requests. If you want to start over, click on the button Play again. But if you want to go out, click on the button Exit

If you want to make the number:

If you want computer to guess the number, click on the button Play versus computer! and guess a number from four non-repeating numbers.
Next, enter the number of "bulls" and "cows" - the result of a computer requests. Bellow will be the history and results of your requests.
If you want to start over, click on the button Play again! But if you want to go out, click on the button Exit
